Silvio Amir

Research Scientist

I research and develop Social Media Analysis systems to draw insights from massive datasets of user generated content. On the technical side, I research and apply Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning and Data Mining methods to extract linguistic signals from social media data. On the methodological side, my work strives to understand what kinds of information can be reliably inferred from this data and identify the best methododologies conduct social media based studies in the real-world. I am particularly interested in applications that can benefit humanity at large, e.g. by enabling more inclusive social and democratic processes, responsive public health interventions tailored to the needs of specific populations, and personalized healthcare. To realize these benefits, I work in multidisciplinary projects that combine human expertise with the capabilities afforded by social media analytics to address pertinent problems in health and social sciences.

Interests

  • Social Media Analysis
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Machine Learning/Deep Learning
  • Information Retrieval and Data Mining
  • Applications in Healthcare, Public Health and the Social Sciences
